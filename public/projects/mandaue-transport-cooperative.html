Who We Are

Rooted in Community, Driven by Purpose

Mandaue Transport Cooperative (MantransCoop) is a registered transport cooperative headquartered in Basak, Mandaue City, Cebu. We are proudly a member of the Federation of Cebu Transport Cooperatives (FCTC), one of 11 cooperatives working together to modernize public transit in Cebu.

Our operations span Cebu City, Mandaue City, Consolacion, and Liloan — connecting thousands of commuters daily with safe, air-conditioned, and GPS-equipped modern jeepneys that comply with the government's Public Utility Vehicle Modernization Program (PUVMP).

"We used to view PUVMP differently, but after experiencing its benefits, our drivers now have decent jobs, stable income, and a sense of community. This is what the cooperative is all about."

— Loreto Gerez, Chairperson, Mandaue Transport Cooperative

What We Offer

Our Services

🚌

Modern PUV Operations

Fully air-conditioned Foton Euro 4 modern jeepneys operating on fixed routes across Cebu, Mandaue, Consolacion, and Liloan. Safe and comfortable rides for every commuter.

💳

Cashless Payments

BEEP Automatic Fare Collection System (AFCS) installed in all our units, enabling fast and secure cashless transactions. No need for exact change — just tap and ride.

📡

Real-Time Tracking

All vehicles are equipped with GPS tracking systems and CCTV cameras ensuring passenger safety and allowing dispatchers to monitor fleet performance in real time.

📶

Free Wi-Fi On Board

Stay connected during your commute. All modern jeepney units are fitted with free Wi-Fi access so you can browse, work, or stay in touch while on the road.

🤝

Driver Welfare Programs

Our members receive government subsidies, access to credit facilities, PhilHealth, SSS, and PAG-IBIG benefits — providing security and dignity to our drivers and operators.

🌿

Eco-Friendly Transport

Euro 4 engine-compliant vehicles that meet environmental standards, reducing emissions and contributing to a cleaner and more sustainable Cebu for future generations.

Join the Cooperative

Become a Member

Join Mandaue Transport Cooperative and be part of a community that empowers drivers, improves livelihoods, and connects Cebu.

01

Inquire & Apply

Visit our office at Basak, Mandaue City or call us to request a membership application form and learn about requirements.

02

Submit Documents

Prepare required documents including valid IDs, proof of PUV ownership or operation, and completed application forms.

03

Orientation & Review

Attend a cooperative orientation session to understand your rights, responsibilities, and the benefits you will receive as a member.

04

Welcome to MTC!

Upon approval and payment of initial share capital, you are officially a member of Mandaue Transport Cooperative.

Government Subsidies — Access to fuel and operational subsidies under the PUVMP program.

Credit Facilities — Low-interest loans for vehicle purchase, repairs, and personal needs through partner institutions like LandBank.

Social Benefits — PhilHealth, SSS, and PAG-IBIG coverage to protect you and your family.

Stable Income — Fixed, regularized income structure replacing boundary-based earnings for greater financial stability.

Voice & Vote — Participate in cooperative governance and decisions that shape your working conditions.

FCTC Network — Be part of the Federation of Cebu Transport Cooperatives and its collective advocacy programs.

Latest Updates

News & Announcements

February 18, 2021

38 Foton Euro 4 Modern Jeepneys Launched in Cebu

In partnership with Cebu CFI Community Cooperative, Mandaue Transport Cooperative was part of the launch of 38 brand-new modern jeepneys at the Cebu Provincial Capitol Grounds. The units began plying routes 13C, 62C, 62B, 13I, and 24G starting March 1, 2021, bringing a new standard of commuter experience to Cebu.

Chairperson Loreto Gerez joins the Federation of Cebu Transport Cooperatives press conference urging unconsolidated jeepney operators to embrace the PUVMP for the benefit of drivers and commuters.
